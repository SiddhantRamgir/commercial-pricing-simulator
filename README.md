# Commercial Pricing Simulator

A decision-focused analytics tool built with Streamlit to model pricing, demand, and cost trade-offs in real time.

This app is designed for commercial stakeholders who need quick, clear answers — not spreadsheets.

---

## Overview

The Commercial Pricing Simulator allows users to adjust key business variables and instantly see their impact on:

- Revenue  
- Gross and Net Profit  
- Profit Margins  
- Break-even Volume  

It reflects how real commercial decisions are made — through trade-offs, not static reports.

---

## Key Features

- **Scenario Modeling**  
  Adjust price, volume, discount, and costs to simulate different business outcomes.

- **Real-Time Insights**  
  Immediate calculation of revenue, margin, and profitability.

- **Break-even Analysis**  
  Understand the minimum volume required to cover costs.

- **Sensitivity Analysis**  
  Visualize how profit changes with price and demand.

- **Scenario Comparison**  
  Compare base, best-case, and worst-case outcomes side by side.

---

## Why This Matters

Most analytics outputs stop at reporting.

This tool focuses on **decision-making**.

It enables non-technical stakeholders to explore commercial strategies independently, reducing reliance on analysts and accelerating planning cycles.
